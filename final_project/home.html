Centuries ago, people didn't have the machines and weights we have today, but people back then were in better shape than those today. Physical growth followed an organic path that was determined by survival needs and by the desire to protect oneself from danger and seize chances for growth. The aim of mental exercises is to maintain and improve cognitive functions including working memory and processing speed.
